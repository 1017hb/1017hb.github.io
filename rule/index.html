#Effective from April 1, 2018

#E – category (entry)

Up to 3000 \

infinite

Installation:

For basic installation requirements only, refer to the matrix on page 8

There is no need to demonstrate.

Documents that only need to be hidden / invisible components.

Restrictions

New immigrants only - competitors have never participated in any Emma or other sanctions body’s sound quality competition

Vehicles without sponsors (all components must be invoiced with proof of purchase upon request)

There are no judges or professionals

There are no modified or self built active components

There are no restrictions on modifying the installation

Competitors can compete for up to two years in the electronic category. However, the winner of the national (and / or international) final must be promoted to a higher level.

#S – category (proficiency)

OEM OEM

Up to 4000 +

infinite

Installation:

See the matrix on page 8 for average installation requirements

There is no need to demonstrate.

Documents that only need to be hidden / invisible components.

Restrictions:

Vehicles without sponsors (all components must be invoiced with proof of purchase upon request)

There are no judges or professionals

There are no modified or self built active components

There are no restrictions on installation modifications except OEM classes

OEM restrictions:

In the “cabin” area, only OEM speaker locations and / or grilles (for one or more speakers) are allowed

Additional / additional speaker positions and / or grilles may also be used if the higher OEM interior level of the competitor’s vehicle has

There are no specially edited car models from the “modification company”; special versions from the original vehicle manufacturer are acceptable

You can change the host or install other hosts

Other volume controls / displays can be installed

Any changes not related to the car audio system are acceptable

Competitors can compete for up to two years in the s category. However, the winner of the national (and / or international) final must be promoted to a higher level.

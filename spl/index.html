#EMMA Sound Pressure League

#Measurement closed:

The first measurement will be with the doors closed – a minimum 30 seconds of music will be played.

Within this measurement, the peak SPL will be measured. Within the next 2 minutes a second measurement will follow:

Measurement open:

The second measurement will be done with both front doors completely open – a minimum 30 seconds of music will be played with the same track as in the first measurement. Within this measurement, the average SPL will be measured.
